ABOUT

Bringing Your Outdoor Dreams to Life: Learn More About Our Passion for Landscaping!

Passionate Landscaping Experts: Lucero

At Lucero Landscaping, we're more than just a business - we're passionate about transforming outdoor spaces into beautiful and functional havens. With a decade of experience and a portfolio of 100+ satisfied clients, we've honed our skills to deliver top-notch services for all your landscaping needs. From designing stunning garden spaces to maintaining lush lawns, we've got you covered. Our team of dedicated professionals is committed to exceeding your expectations and creating a landscape that you'll love for years to come. Trust us to bring your vision to life!

Services

We provide a complete list of landscape & hardscape needs.

Trimming

Transform your landscaping with a deep summer pruning that promotes healthy plant and tree growth while keeping your yard neatly shaped year-round

Thatching & Aeration

Achieve a healthy, lush lawn with our expert dethatching in spring and core aeration in fall, ensuring your yard looks vibrant all year.

Drainage & Re-grading

Let us transform your yard with a proper drainage system that maximizes its beauty and functionality, solving any water problems for good.

Mulching

Enhance your plant beds with our fresh Cedar, Hemlock, and Bark mulch options in blonde, brown, and red, providing a beautiful complement to your yard.

Weekly Grass Cutting

Keep your yard in perfect shape with our weekly or bi-weekly services, including thorough edging, weed removal, and grass clipping disposal.

Seeding

Whether you need general over-seeding, brand new seeded lawn, or complete weed-free sod installation, we have you covered.

Ice Control

Stay safe during the winter with our comprehensive snow removal and ice control services, keeping your walkways and driveways slip-free

Fence Installation

Upgrade your yard with beautiful, functional fencing options like vinyl, wooden, and chain link to achieve the look and privacy you desire

Retaining Walls

Add dimension and stability to your yard with our expertly designed retaining walls, from simple garden blocks to large-scale solutions tailored to your needs.
